Rules and Regulations

1. Men and women from all walks of life can participate in these competitions.

2. One participant can participate in all the competitions for which one needs to fill different paid applications.

3. For the classical competitions, accompanist is mandatory. Do Not perform on sound print. Can be relaxed for the dance competitions.

4. For the classical performances, 1-15 minutes of time would be allotted as per the age group. It would be disclosed 2 hours prior to the competition.

5. Songs-gazals-bhajans can be sung under the category of light music. There is no language barrier.

6. For film music, the orchestra would be available in the centre. Use of karaoke is also allowed.

7. In the first round of film music, the participant requires to sing just one stanza. In the second round, the entire song has to be sung.

8. Judges’ decision would be final.

9. All the decisions would be secured by the centre.

Note- *prize-trophy, certificate as well as a secret-reward would be awarded, which would be disclosed on the first day of the competitions.
